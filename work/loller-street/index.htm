Loller

Client
Upscale Property
Typology
Multi-Residential
Year
2025
Status
Completed
01
Project details

Situated in the heart of Brighton, 1 Loller as a collection of six residences epitomise luxury living. With an emphasis on honest materiality and distinctive identity, both the architectural and interior language provide an exclusive lifestyle offering.
Country
Bunurong / Boon Wurrung
Collaborators
Mim Design (Interior Design)
Paul Bangay (Landscape Design)
Edge (Structural Engineer)
ESD (Ark Resources)
Timothy Kaye (Photography)
Residences
6
Discipline
Architecture

Inspired by Le Corbusier's Dom-ino House to maximise layout flexibility.
02
About the project

Responding to the local context, the architectural facade treatment provides a strong sense of address with a residentially scaled rhythm to the street interface, with feature hand-rendered columns puncturing the slab edge.

03
About the project

Influenced by modernist ideas, external rows of round columns creating moments of verticality and low brick banding allow the exteriors to feel like an extension of interior living zones, all softened by the surrounding lush landscaping.
04
About the project

The interior expression heroes high end finishes with sculptural forms. Each home has been carefully crafted to include all the amenity of a house within each residence.
05
About the project

With special consideration into all appliances, fixtures, materials and spatial configuration, Loller Street Residences exude enduring quality. Polished chrome columns internally designed by Mim Design echo honest materiality and modernist sensibility.
