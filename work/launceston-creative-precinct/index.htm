02
About the Project

Inspired by natural rock formations, the design integrates a double height folding colonnade, bold brick elements and sculptural tower features, creating a harmonious blend that complements the existing streetscapes and neighborhoods of the surrounding site.

03
About the Project

The overall precinct offers a diverse array of amenities, including hotel, versatile function spaces, wellness facilities, public transit access, retail, food and beverage and office spaces.
04
About the Project

Embracing the heritage listed building is balanced with integrating a contemporary approach to establish a public arcade, pedestrian walkway and landscaped areas that enhance connectivity, natural light and separation between the heritage and modern structures.
